### Important CSV files in original condition

`train_zero_shot.csv`:<br>
This is the dataset to train the models on in the one-shot setting.

`train_one_shot.csv`:<br>
This is the dataset to train the models on in the one-shot setting.

`test.csv`:<br>
These are the samples (without) labels to evaluate the trained models on (both settings). Originally named `dev.csv`.
 
`test_gold.csv`:<br>
These are the labels to the samples in `dev.csv`. Originally named `dev_gold.csv`.
